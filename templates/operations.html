Mode

The mode or modal value of a data set is the most frequently occurring value. It’s a measure of central tendency that tells you the most popular choice or most common characteristic of your sample. When reporting descriptive statistics, measures of central tendency help you find the middle or the average of your data set. i.e Age: 19
